Brief

We keep building our range in specialty papers always seeking for
the best available solutions in the market. Our range includes papers
used in the construction industry such as plasterboard liner as well as
coreboard offered to core and tube manufacturers in many
industries.

Grade:
  • Gypsum liner

Properties: High tensile strength securing high flexural strength
the final board, Proper sizing that insures a flat smooth board
surface

Application: Gypsum plasterboard for interior wall lining systems

Grade:
  • Coreboard

Properties: High ply-bond, stiffness, and ring crush properties providing stronger paper tubes, High tensile strength securing high speed run-ability on the machine.

Application: Cores and tubes for paper, plastic film, aluminum,
Tapes, Textiles,Composite cans etc.
