Business / M&A Brokerage

Our Business / M&A brokerage service include

We mainly focus on projects within the paper & packaging industry matching companies that are interested in expanding their activities/footprint in SE Europe with successful business owners and corporate management who would like to sell their company. The whole procedure is executed with strict confidentiality and professionalism to achieve the best price for both parties. Our company specializes in valuing, marketing and facilitating transactions of small to medium-size businesses with revenues between $2M and $50M.
