About Us

ELTRACOM Ltd is a privately-owned independent paper and paperboard agency founded in April 2014 from experienced professionals in the field of paper and corrugated packaging industry since 1983.

The company represents a number of well-known paper and packaging groups and it is specialized in the sales of corrugated case materials (recycled and virgin fiber), duplex coated paperboard

(recycled and virgin fiber), flexible packaging papers, heavy duty corrugated containers, etc.

Despite the very short time since its establishment, the company has achieved to be ranked among the top Greek agencies in the said business field.

Recently, our company entered the M&A brokerage services focusing exclusively on the paper & packaging industry for projects related to the region of SE Europe.
